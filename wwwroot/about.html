About Record Rack

Background

This generation of music listeners have the privilege of accessing the medium through different platforms. This includes streaming services like Spotify, physical media such as CDs/vinyl records, or independent websites like Soundcloud.. Record Rack aims to serve as a centralized web app that allows users to keep track of all their music, accessible from anywhere.

Issues/Feature Requests

If there is any feature you'd like added to Record Rack, or you find a fatal bug or error, feel free to open an issue on the app's Github page.
